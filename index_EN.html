Objetive

The "Moshi Msamaria Association" is committed to improving the lives of 100 orphaned children in Tanzania, specifically in the Moshi region. Our mission is to provide them with a safe home, access to quality education, medical care, and emotional support, so they can grow and thrive in a conducive environment.

Vision

We strive to create a future full of hope and opportunities for the underprivileged children of Moshi. We aim to be a beacon of love and care in their lives, providing them with the necessary tools to become resilient leaders and contributors in their communities.

Impact

Since our foundation, we have transformed the lives of numerous children in Moshi, providing them with love, education, and hope for a better future. Our alumni have continued their studies and have positively contributed to their communities, demonstrating the transformative power of proper care and support.

Values

  • Compassion

    We deeply care about the well-being of each child and strive to understand and address their individual needs.

  • Integrity

    We operate with transparency and honesty in all our actions, ensuring the trust of our donors and the community at large.

  • Dedication

    We are committed to providing continuous, high-quality care to the children under our guardianship, no matter the challenges we face.

  • Collaboration

    We work closely with local authorities, other organizations, and the community at large to maximize the positive impact of our interventions.

    • Programs and Activities

  • Comprehensive Care

    We provide a family-like and secure environment for the children, where they have access to food, housing, and medical care.

  • Education

    We ensure that all children have access to quality education, providing them with opportunities to develop their academic and personal potential.

  • Health and Well-being

    We provide preventive and curative medical care, as well as psychological support, to promote the overall health of the children.

  • Community Developmen

    We work on projects that benefit not only the children in our orphanage but also the community at large, fostering a sense of belonging and solidarity.

    • How can your contribution help?

  • You will help ensure that children have access to quality education, including school fees, educational materials, and extracurricular programs.

  • Your donation can finance necessary medical care, medications, and preventive health services to ensure the physical and emotional well-being of the children.

  • Your donation can support psychological and emotional support programs for children who have experienced trauma or emotional difficulties.

  • You will contribute to projects that benefit not only the children in the orphanage but also the community at large, such as the construction of water wells, community nutrition programs, or local economic development initiatives.

    Your donation will have a direct and tangible impact on the lives of vulnerable children in the orphanage. You will contribute to providing them with food, housing, education, and medical care, giving them opportunities for a better future.
